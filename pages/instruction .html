Memory Game!

Instrucion:

1. Choose level for your game:
easy- 4X4
level- 6X6
2. Which step open two cards and check if they are the same or different
3. If the cards are different, try to remember where each card is
because they close after 2 seconds
4. If the cards are the same, they will remain open and you will earn points
5. In order to win you have to play as quickly as possible and open as few cards as possible
so you will accumulate a lot of points
6. The game is over after 2 minuts.
Have Fun!
